What we do

Integrated design & build solutions

At Archevo Infra Edge Pvt Ltd, we blend structural expertise with aesthetic precision — functional, compliant, and beautifully designed spaces from foundation to finish.

01 — Architecture

Shell & strategy

Site analysis, zoning compliance, volumetric studies, façade design, and working drawings coordinated with structural and MEP consultants.

We optimise orientation, shading, and cross-ventilation before ornament enters the conversation — especially critical for Gujarat’s climate.

02 — Interiors

Atmosphere & detail

Spatial planning, lighting layers, bespoke joinery, material palettes, FF&E schedules, and styling — documented for tender and execution.

Every elevation and section is coordinated with services so outlets, diffusers, and details align with the visual rhythm.

03 — Delivery

Execution you can trust

BOQs, vendor shortlists, site visits, mock-up reviews, snag lists, and handover documentation — we protect design intent through construction.

Optional turnkey packages include procurement and installation supervision for clients who want a single point of contact.
