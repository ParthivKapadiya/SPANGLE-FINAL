Workplaces that perform

What hybrid teams need from physical space — beyond the desk farm.

Published November 2025 · Archevo Design

The best offices today behave like hospitality: clear arrival, good coffee, daylight at desks, and real acoustic privacy for calls. Employees vote with attendance — if the office is worse than home, they stay home.

Zones, not open plans

We design neighbourhoods: focus rooms for two, phone booths, team tables, and generous social kitchens. Circulation is widened so chance encounters feel generous, not cramped.

Brand without billboards

Corporate identity appears in materiality and lighting — not only in wall graphics. A restrained palette lets teams project their own culture onto the space.
